# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 22, 2026  
**Product:** FastDoc (“FastDoc”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) — a browser extension and related services for AI-assisted clinical documentation and EMR-related workflows.

This Privacy Policy describes how FastDoc collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the FastDoc browser extension and any websites or APIs we operate that are linked from the extension (together, the **“Services”**).

If you are using FastDoc on behalf of a clinic, practice, or other organization, that organization’s policies may also apply.

---

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Information you provide

- **Account and profile information**, such as name, email address, professional role or specialty, clinic or organization name, and credentials used to sign in.
- **Clinical workflow content you choose to submit**, which may include patient demographics or identifiers you enter, encounter metadata, transcripts, recordings, notes, and other documentation you generate or upload through the Services.
- **Support communications**, such as messages you send to us (for example, via `support@fastdoc.app`).

### 1.2 Information collected automatically

- **Device and technical data**, such as browser type, extension version, operating system, and general diagnostic or error information needed to operate and improve the Services.
- **Usage data**, such as feature usage, session timestamps, and performance metrics, where enabled.
- **Local storage on your device**, where the extension may store preferences, tokens, or cached content using browser storage capabilities you authorize.

### 1.3 Audio and microphone

Where you grant permission, the extension may access your **microphone** to capture audio for transcription and documentation features. Audio may be processed locally and/or transmitted to our servers and third-party processors to provide transcription, summarization, and related AI features.

### 1.4 Tabs and browsing context

Where enabled by permissions and your workflow, the extension may interact with browser tabs to support documentation features (for example, to assist with EMR-related workflows). We aim to collect only what is reasonably necessary to provide the requested functionality.

## 2. How we use information

We use information to:

- Provide, maintain, secure, and improve the Services (including AI-assisted documentation).
- Authenticate users, enforce access controls (including organization or clinic scoping where configured), and prevent fraud or abuse.
- Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
- Comply with law and protect rights, safety, and property.
- Generate de-identified or aggregated analytics where permitted.

We do not sell your personal information as a standalone product.

## 3. Health information

Depending on how you use FastDoc, information processed through the Services may include **protected health information (PHI)** under applicable regulations (for example, HIPAA in the United States).

- If you are a **covered entity** or **business associate** under HIPAA, additional agreements (such as a Business Associate Agreement) may be required before PHI may be used or disclosed in certain ways. You are responsible for determining whether PHI is permitted to be processed in your environment and for configuring access controls consistent with your policies.
- You should only submit the minimum information necessary for your clinical workflow.
